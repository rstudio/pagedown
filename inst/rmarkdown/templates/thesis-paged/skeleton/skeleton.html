Abstract

Reproducible thesis documentation is a rarity in academia. Often graduate student’s are given little choice when it comes to how their thesis will be written, this is almost always in MS Word. The {badgerdown} package is an attempt to free said students from the dred that comes along with any MS Word document with > 7 pages long.
