1 A Minimal Thesis Template

Please refer to pagedown::html_thesis to see a full example of how the thesis template can be utilized. For example it shows how to us a seperate .Rmd file for each chapter of your thesis so you can focus on them individually.

This template was created by Brent Thorne and was inspired by his inability to cope with writting a master’s thesis in MS Word.
